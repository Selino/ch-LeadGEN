# Colors

Keep in mind that the effect of your branding colors depends on the style and design they are used in, as well as the color combinations you choose.

- Green evokes stability, prosperity, growth and a connection to nature.
- A light shade of blue exudes tranquility, trust, openness. It can also signify innocence.
- Dark blue stands for professionalism, security and formality. It is mature and trustworthy.
